Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Cafe Bella

How far in advance should I book?
Bookings must be made at least one (1) week in advance.
Is there a reservation fee?
A ₱2,000 reservation fee is required to secure the booking.
What payment methods do you accept?
Payments accepted: GCash and Cash.
When should the remaining balance be paid?
The remaining balance must be settled before or on the event date.
Is the reservation fee refundable?
The reservation fee is non-refundable.
Can I cancel my booking?
No cancellation policy. Once a booking is confirmed, it cannot be canceled.
What information should I provide when booking?
Clients must provide accurate event details.
Can Cafe Bella change my booking?
Cafe BELLA may adjust bookings due to unforeseen circumstances.
