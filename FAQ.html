Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Cafe Bella

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 1 week in advance to secure your preferred date and time.
Is there a reservation fee?
A ₱2,000 reservation fee is required to secure your booking slot.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept GCash and Cash payments.
When should the remaining balance be paid?
The remaining balance must be settled before or on the event date.
Is the reservation fee refundable?
The reservation fee is strictly non-refundable once the booking is confirmed.
Can I cancel my booking?
No cancellation policy. Once confirmed, bookings cannot be canceled.
What information should I provide when booking?
Clients must provide complete and accurate event details including date, location, and package selection.
Can Cafe Bella change my booking?
Cafe BELLA may adjust bookings due to unforeseen circumstances, but clients will be notified immediately.
