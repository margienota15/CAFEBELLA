Frequently ask questions

Everything you need to know about our event booking services
How early should I book and is there a reservation fee? ▼

Bookings must be made at least one (1) week in advance.
A ₱2,000 reservation fee is required to secure your booking.

What are the payment methods and when should I pay the balance? ▼

We accept GCash and Cash payments.
The remaining balance must be settled before or on the event date.

Can I cancel my booking or get a refund for the reservation fee? ▼

Yes! We offer customizable packages depending on your event needs.
